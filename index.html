About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at the University of California, Irvine. Padhraic Smyth is my advisor. My research interest is in probabilistic machine learning and statistics. In particular, I am interstested in understainding uncertainty and robustness of deep learning and probabilistic models, and their applications to medical data.

I've done internships at Google Cambridge and Facebook New York. I graduated from Department of Mathematics at Fudan University (Shanghai, China) in 2015.
